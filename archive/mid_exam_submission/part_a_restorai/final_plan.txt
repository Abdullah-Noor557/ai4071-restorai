🔧 **STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS**  
──────────────────────────────  
Step 1: Clean the surface with mineral spirits to remove any dirt and grease.  
Step 2: Repair extensive finish loss by filling deep scratches with colored wood filler or shellac stick, then level and sand.  
Step 3: Reattach the detached leg by cleaning the joint surfaces, applying yellow wood glue, and clamping for 24 hours.  
Step 4: Inspect the veneer for any lifting or damage; reattach loose veneer with yellow wood glue and clamp.  
Step 5: Apply a new finish to the entire surface, ensuring even coverage.  

⚠️ **SAFETY WARNINGS**  
───────────────────  
• NO SANDING on veneer surfaces.  
• Work in a well-ventilated area when using chemical strippers.  
• Wear chemical-resistant gloves and goggles during the restoration process.
